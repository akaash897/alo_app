Our Story

ALo was born from a passion for creating moments of tranquility in our busy urban lives. Each candle is handcrafted with care in Kolkata, using only the finest natural ingredients and essential oils inspired by India's rich aromatic heritage.

Our mission is to bring warmth, comfort, and a touch of luxury to your everyday life at affordable prices. We believe in sustainable practices and eco-friendly materials, ensuring our products are as kind to the earth as they are to your senses.

Meet the Founder

"I started ALo while pursuing my nursing studies, with a simple vision: to create candles that would transform everyday spaces into sanctuaries of calm and healing."

As a nursing student at PG Hospital, I understand the importance of creating restorative environments. My passion for aromatherapy and traditional Indian fragrances led me to develop ALo candles—combining my knowledge of wellness with beautiful scent profiles that promote relaxation and rejuvenation.

— Sreya Mondal, Founder
