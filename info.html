Shipping & Returns

Processing Time

All orders are handcrafted and packed with care. Please allow 2–3 business days for processing before dispatch. Orders placed on weekends or public holidays are processed the next working day.

Shipping Rates & Delivery

Delivery timelines are estimates and may vary due to courier delays or remote locations. We currently ship only within India.

Packaging

Every ALo candle is wrapped in recycled tissue paper and nestled in a kraft gift box with a personalised note — ready to gift straight from the box. We avoid single-use plastics wherever possible.

Returns & Refunds

Your satisfaction is our priority. We accept returns under the following conditions:

  • Item received damaged or defective — contact us within 48 hours of delivery with a photo.
  • Wrong item sent — we will arrange a free replacement or full refund.
  • Due to the handcrafted and perishable nature of our products, we do not accept returns for change of mind.

To initiate a return, email us at hello@alocandles.com with your order number and issue. Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days of approval.
